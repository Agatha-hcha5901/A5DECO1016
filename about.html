    WHAT WE DO

    DISASTERS RELIEF

    Every year, natural disasters destroy local economies and devastate lives. They cause Americans emotional damage and cost $24.4 billion in insured losses each year. Earthquakes, hurricanes, tornadoes, wildfires, droughts, floods, and other natural disasters pose a great threat to our country. We’re here to help – in any given year, we provide nearly 800,000 survivors with support, hope, and healing.

    FOOD SHORTAGE

    An additional 6.9 million families live on the verge of food insecurity. That means they don’t always know where their next meal is coming from. During the pandemic, the number sky-rocket.
